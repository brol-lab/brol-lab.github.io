Directions

Expanded descriptions (details live here, not on Home).

Policy Learning

Reinforcement and imitation learning for real robots: sample efficiency, stability, and generalization under partial observability. We explore offline learning, feedback-driven learning, and scalable evaluation.

Offline RL Feedback / Preferences Generalization

Manipulation & Control

Contact-rich manipulation with vision/tactile sensing. We combine learning with planning and classical control to achieve reliable grasping and dexterity on real hardware.

Dexterity Tactile / Vision Planning

Reliability & Evaluation

Robustness, uncertainty, monitoring, and safety. We build evaluation protocols and benchmarks that reflect real-world distribution shift and non-stationarity.

OOD Uncertainty Monitoring
